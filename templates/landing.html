The open-source tool for intuitive Game Mastering

Welcome to GM App

GM App is your ultimate companion for running immersive and dynamic pen-and-paper RPG sessions. Designed for Game Masters, it helps you react intuitively and creatively to unexpected events, ensuring every session feels seamless and engaging. With GM App, you can generate encounters, improvise NPCs, and adapt the storyline on the fly—making spontaneous moments more exciting and effortless. Whether you're managing combat, crafting side quests, or handling unpredictable player actions, this app keeps you prepared without breaking immersion.

No matter your experience level, GM App empowers you to run smooth and captivating games with minimal preparation. Stay in control, fuel your creativity, and enhance your storytelling—all in one intuitive tool.

Stay flexible, stay creative, and bring your world to life!
